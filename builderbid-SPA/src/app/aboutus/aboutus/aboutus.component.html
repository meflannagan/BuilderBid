Who We Are

BuilderBid is the collaborative effort of three Chattanooga State students who saw a deficiency in the legacy traditional contractor budding process.. Coming from diverse backgrounds, each student brings his own unique talents to the project.

Our Method

We create healthy working relationships between general and subcontractors by providing an open auction biding process and social profiles to promote services. We accomplish this through our builderbid.us website. This site is a central communication hub which combines all the benefits of social networking in a professional environment.

Get Results

Studies have shown that open public auctions create a balanced and fair process for selling products and services. By the same token, open auctions also create lower bids for the same quality work, saving money for general contractors.
